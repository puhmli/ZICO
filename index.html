Research Statement

我的研究围绕食品系统中的化学污染与食源性疾病风险，构建从监测数据、污染发生概率、膳食暴露、健康经济损失到监管策略优化的量化框架。方法上结合概率暴露建模、贝叶斯网络、整数规划、多准则决策分析与深度强化学习，目标是把风险科学转化为监管部门、国际组织和食品供应链主体可执行的决策支持工具。

My research develops quantitative frameworks that connect monitoring data, contaminant occurrence, dietary exposure, health-economic losses, and optimized regulatory strategies for chemical and foodborne risks in food systems. Methodologically, I combine probabilistic exposure modeling, Bayesian networks, integer programming, multi-criteria decision analysis, and deep reinforcement learning to translate risk science into actionable decision-support tools for regulators, international organizations, and food supply-chain actors.
